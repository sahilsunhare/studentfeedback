About IGTR, Indore

MSME TECHNOLOGY CENTRE, INDORE (Indo German Tool Room) is one of the leading tool rooms in central part of India.It is located at Indore, one of the commercial cities of Madhya Pradesh. It is a Central Govt. project with assistance from Republic of Germany. Government of Madhya Pradesh provided land, building & infrastructure. It is providing services to various customers in below mentioned fields.

Design and manufacturing of Press Tools, Moulds and Die casting die.
Design and manufacturing of Jigs, Fixtures and Gauges
Job work in CNC Machining.
Mass and Pilot Production of Precision Components/ Assemblies /Sub-Assemblies
We have developed our capacity and capability for manufacturing of
Special type of Magnets used in Electron Beam Accelerator required for R&D Labs. Special Moulds like Form Fill and Blow Fill moulds for Pharmaceutical Industries used in liquid medicine packaging.
