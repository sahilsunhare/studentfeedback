Apply for D – Voc programmes in Kerala before August 20

Three-year Diploma in Vocation (D – Voc) programs are held at the five government polytechnic colleges in Kerala. These courses that have the approval of the All Indian Council of Technical Education are known for their incredible employment scope.

» Government Polytechnic, Attingal: There are 60 seats up for grabs for Automobile Servicing while 60 seats are available for Hospitality Management.

» Government Polytechnic, Nattakam, Kottayam: 60 seats for Automobile Servicing.

» Maharajas Technological Institute, Thrissur: There are 30 seats for Electronics Manufacturing Services.

» Institute of Printing Technology, Shoranur: 30 seats are up for grabs for Printing Technology.

» Government Polytechnic, Perinthalmanna: 30 seats for Electronics Manufacturing Services.

Eligibility

Those who have passed Secondary School Leaving Certificate Exam / Technical High School Leaving Certificate Examination can apply. Admission would be based on the grades score in the qualifying exam. There is no entrance exam. The classes will be held on six days in a week from 2 pm – 7 pm. The annual tuition fee is Rs 37,500.

The application forms could be downloaded from the website. The filled–in applications should be submitted at the respective colleges on or before 20 August. There is no application fee. The prospectus too is available on the website. Website: www.polyadmission.org/dvoc; www.asapkerala.gov.in.
